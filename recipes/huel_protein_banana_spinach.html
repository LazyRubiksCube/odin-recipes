Ingredients

  • 1 scoop from your Huel Banana Pudding Protein
  • 3 oz. spinach
  • 11 oz. oat milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter

Steps

  1. Add 11 oz. of oat milk to blender
  2. Add half spinach and banana and begin blending.
  3. Add peanut butter and remaining half of spinach and banana. Continue blending till smooth.
  4. Add in 1 scoop of your Huel Banana Pudding Protein powder. Blend till smooth.
  5. Pour into cup and enjoy!
