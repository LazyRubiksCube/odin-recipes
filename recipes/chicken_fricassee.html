Chicken Fricassee

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 lb. skin-on, bone-in chicken thighs or chicken breast
  • kosher salt
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp. unsalted butter
  • 1 larg onion, chopped
  • 1 large carrot, peeled and chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tbsp, all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 c. white wine
  • 2 c. low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 c. heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp. freshly chopped parsley
  • 2 tsp. freshly thyme leaves

Steps

  1. Heat large skillet on medium heat with oil
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper on both sides and add to skillet, cooking for 5 minutes per side. Remove and set aside
  3. Add and melt butter in skillet then add vegetables and cook until soft or 5 minutes.
  4. Add flour and cook for 1 minute. Pour in wine and srape up any bits from bottom of pan, reduce half
  5. Add broth and cream, season with parsley, thyme, and salt & pepper. Add chicken back into pan and simmer until chicken is cooked through and sauce has thickened ~15 minutes
