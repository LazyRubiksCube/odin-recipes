Ingredients

  • 3/4 c. old-fashioned rolled oats
  • coconut
  • 1 tbsp. chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp. shredded, sweetened coconut
  • 1 tbsp. maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp. unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 c. chocolate-flavored almond milk

Steps

  1. Mix almond milk, oats, chia seeds, coconut powder, and vanilla extract into a 12 ounce mason jar.
  2. Cover and refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight.
  3. Stir and serve cold.
